Global Brigades Canada

Our Holistic Model addresses the interconnected root causes of poverty through six pillars. Select a segment on the wheel to learn more about each programme area.

Each pillar reinforces the others — economic empowerment supports health outcomes, clean infrastructure enables education, and legal access protects all of it. Together, they build communities that thrive independently and sustainably.

Health & Well-being

We partner with communities to expand access to medical, dental, and preventative care while fostering long-term wellness, education, and psychosocial support.

Aligned Programs: Student-led brigades (Medical and Dental), Faculty-led programs (Kambia Nursing and Medical Squads), and community-led initiatives (Community Health Workers) synergistically strengthen local health systems and empower healthier futures.

Sustainability Lens: Training local health workers ensures continuity of care, health education reduces preventable disease through long-lasting systems, and our clinics integrate environmentally responsible practices that minimize waste and protect community environments.

Economic Empowerment

We create pathways out of poverty by promoting inclusive finance, entrepreneurship, and affordable housing — building stability that communities can sustain for generations.

Aligned Programs: Student-led brigades (Global Business Consulting, Empowered Legal), Faculty-led programs (Kambia Business), and community-led initiatives like Eskala and Montemar enable families to access capital, build businesses, and achieve homeownership.

Sustainability Lens: Eskala's community-owned banks are designed for longevity, Montemar's eco-friendly homes balance affordability with environmental resilience, and business advising encourages growth strategies that protect natural resources and prioritize circular economies.

Sustainable Infrastructure

We co-create the physical foundations every community needs — clean water, sanitation, housing, renewable energy, and resilient public spaces.

Aligned Programs: Student-led brigades (Global Engineering Consulting, Public Health Brigades and Water Brigades) and Faculty-led programs (STEM Squads and Kambia Engineering) design and implement immediate solutions, while community-led programs like Reforestation & Agroforestry and Montemar expand long-term environmental resilience.

Sustainability Lens: Our engineers design climate-resilient water and sanitation systems, eco-housing construction integrates renewable energy and resource conservation, and reforestation restores local ecosystems to ensure infrastructure development goes hand-in-hand with environmental stewardship.
